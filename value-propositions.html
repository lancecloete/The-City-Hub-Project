Value Propositions

1.Target Customers

Small and Medium business owners.

2. Challenges Addressed

  • Difficulty sourcing high quality, affordable fresh products from reliable suppliers.
  • Fragmented supplier landscape, inconsistent quality and inefficient procurement process.
  • Lack of transparency in pricing and terms.

    • 3. Elements of the Solution

  • Centralized digital platform connecting businesses with verified suppliers.
  • Supplier directory with profiles, ratings and certification.
  • Bulk ordering platform with discount incentives and communication forums.
  • Quality assurance to ensure product pricing and quality.

    • 4. Customer Value

  • Save time and funds by streamlining procurement.
  • Improved product quality and consistency.
  • Bulk supply and discount incentives
  • Build stronger relations with reliable suppliers.

    • 5. Societal Value

  • Support economic development by connecting business with local suppliers.
  • Promote sustainable and ethical sourcing practices.
  • Enhance security and safety through consistent supply of quality products.
